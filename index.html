AnalogChannel

a VST3 channel strip plugin designed for mixing engineers who demand fast and characterful results in their workflow

Free Download

100% Free - Support is optional but appreciated :)

Key features:

  • 8 Processing sections with carefully limited controls, for fast and colorful results
  • Channel Variation System - Emulates natural component tolerances in analog consoles with 48 unique channel presets
  • Dual-Mono Architecture
  • Independent section Bypass with resource optimization
  • Flexible Signal Routing
  • Full DAW Automation
  • Zero Latency
  • 100% Free & Open Source
