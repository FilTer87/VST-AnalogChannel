# AnalogChannel

**Professional 8-Section Channel Strip Plugin for Modern Mixing**

AnalogChannel is a comprehensive VST3 channel strip that brings classic analog console workflow to your DAW. Designed for mix engineers who want studio-grade processing with intuitive controls and legendary analog character.

## Features

- **Pre-Input Saturation** - Add color and warmth with four saturation modes: Clean, Pure (transparent), Tape (vintage flutter and head bump), and Tube (asymmetric harmonics)

- **Surgical Filters** - Precision high-pass and low-pass filters (12-48 dB/oct) for cleaning up your mix without sacrificing tone

- **Control Compressor** - Transparent peak control for taming dynamics before EQ, ensuring clean and consistent levels

- **4-Band EQ** - Musical shelving and parametric EQ with API-style dynamic Q for surgical cuts and broad boosts

- **Style Compressor** - Two character compression modes: Warm (optical CL1B-style) and Punch (versatile FET-style) for adding vibe and glue

- **Console Emulation** - Classic analog console tones: Essex (Neve warmth), Oxford (SSL precision), and USA (API punch)

- **Output Stage** - Final saturation and clipping options (Hard/Soft) for controlled loudness and analog-style limiting

- **Master Volume** - Final gain control with precision metering for optimal gain staging

Built with faithful ports of legendary algorithms from Airwindows and classic JSFX processors. Dual-mono architecture for authentic analog modeling and future TMT-style channel variation.
